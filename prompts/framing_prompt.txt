   - 1.3: Contract Lifecycle Management
   - 1.4: Data Room & Due Diligence Coordination

2. CEO Office & Governance (CEO Ofisi ve Yönetişim)
   - 2.1: CEO Office Setup & Operating Rhythm
   - 2.2: Authority Matrix & Approval Workflows
   - 2.3: OKR/KPI Architecture
   - 2.4: Executive Reporting & Risk Governance

3. Development Finance & Compliance (Kalkınma Finansmanı ve Uyum)
   - 3.1: IFI/DFI Relationship Management
   - 3.2: Requirements Library & Traceability
   - 3.3: Lender-Grade Reporting Factory
   - 3.4: Covenant Monitoring & Compliance

4. Project Development & Finance (Proje Geliştirme ve Finansman)
   - 4.1: Feasibility Studies & Roadmap
   - 4.2: Site Assessment & Permitting
   - 4.3: Project Finance Structuring
   - 4.4: Financial Modeling & Value Engineering

5. Engineering & Delivery (Mühendislik ve Teslimat)
   - 5.1: FEED & Detailed Design Management
   - 5.2: Procurement & Vendor Governance
   - 5.3: EPC & Construction Management
   - 5.4: Commissioning & Performance Testing

6. Asset Management (O&M) (Varlık Yönetimi)
   - 6.1: O&M Governance & SLA Management
   - 6.2: Performance Monitoring & Optimization
   - 6.3: Predictive Maintenance Strategy
   - 6.4: Modernization & Decommissioning

7. GTM & JV Management (Pazara Giriş ve Ortak Girişim)
   - 7.1: Market Entry Strategy & Execution
   - 7.2: Partner Development & Selection
   - 7.3: JV Setup & Governance Controls
   - 7.4: Commercial Frameworks & KPIs

8. Digital Platforms (Dijital Platformlar)
   - 8.1: ERP & Enterprise Digitization
   - 8.2: PMIS & PPM Implementation
   - 8.3: Data & AI Enablement
